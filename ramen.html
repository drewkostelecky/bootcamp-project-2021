Ingredients:

  • 1 pack of your favorite ramen
  • 2 slices of bacon
  • 2 eggs
  • green onion

Procedure

  1. Bring water to a boil
  2. Boil ramen noodles for 2 minutes
  3. On a skillet, fry bacon until cooked to preference
  4. Fry eggs to preference
  5. Cut up bacon
  6. Throw seasoning packet, bacon and eggs into ramen broth
  7. Top with chopped up green onion
  8. Enjoy!
