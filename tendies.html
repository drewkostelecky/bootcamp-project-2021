Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 2 Tbsp Frank's RedHot Seasoning
  • 1/2 pound chicken tenders
  • 1/8 cup milk

Procedure

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Pat chicken tenders dry
  3. Pour milk into a bowl
  4. In a shallow dish, combine the panko and the seasoning
  5. Dip chicken tenders into milk one at a time
  6. Dip the chicken tenders into the panko and seasoning mix
  7. Place chicken tenders onto a baking sheet
  8. Bake in the oven for 18-20 minutes
  9. Pair with your favorite vegetable and enjoy!
