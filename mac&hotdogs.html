Ingredients:

  • 1 box kraft mac and cheese
  • 2 beef franks
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 Tbsp butter

Procedure

  1. Bring water in pot to a boil
  2. Boil macaroni for 6-7 minutes
  3. Mix cheese packet, milk, and butter into macaroni
  4. Cook hot dogs on a skillet
  5. Cut up hotdogs and stir into macaroni
  6. Enjoy!
